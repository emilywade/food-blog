RED HOT CHILI PEPPERS

About the Band

Red Hot Chili Peppers (commonly abbreviated as RHCP) are an American rock band formed in Los Angeles in 1983. Their music incorporates elements of alternative rock, funk, punk rock and psychedelic rock. The band comprises vocalist Anthony Kiedis, bassist Flea, drummer Chad Smith, and guitarist John Frusciante. With over 80 million records sold worldwide, Red Hot Chili Peppers are one of the best-selling bands of all time.

Few Fun Facts

  • Blood Sugar Sex Magik was recorded in Harry Houdini's old haunted mansion
  • There is a "Bridge" that "Under the Bridge" was based on
  • Over the Red Hot Chili Peppers 29 year tenure, Anthony Kiedis and Flea have been the only consistent members
